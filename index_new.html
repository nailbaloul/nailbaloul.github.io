About Me

Since October 2024, I am a PhD student at the CERMICS laboratory (École nationale des ponts et chaussées). My research focuses on traffic flow modeling, inverse problems, and learning-based methods for PDE-constrained systems. I am supervised by Pierre Lissy (Junior Professor, ENPC) and Thibault Liard.
